Knowledge Base & FAQs

What is the best CCTV camera to buy for home?

For homes, we recommend CP Plus or Hikvision WiFi cameras. If you want 360¬∞ coverage, a dome camera is best. For outdoor security, bullet cameras with high-definition night vision are ideal.

Why is my CCTV camera not working at night?

Common reasons include infrared (IR) failure, low power supply, or dirty lenses. We provide CCTV camera repair in Unnao to fix these issues onsite.

Are CCTV cameras allowed in the workplace?

Yes, surveillance in government offices and private workplaces is common for security. Ensure you have a CCTV cameras in operation sign visible.

What does a CCTV camera red light meaning?

A steady red light usually means the infrared (IR) LEDs are active for night vision. If it's flashing, it could indicate a recording status or network issue.
